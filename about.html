Design Tenets

  • Two-page footprint to minimize surface area and keep focus on the prompts.

  • Static hosting friendly; all orchestration happens in the browser.
  • Repository-backed prompts for auditability and collaborative iteration.

Safety Intent

"Every simulated breach is a rehearsal for resilience. Keep logs, honour policy, and never test tactics on production systems."

When you arm the console, credentials stay in local storage and are masked everywhere else. Sessions can be purged at any time. Chat conversations run against your chosen OpenRouter model with the selected prompt injected as the system message, so document mitigations as you iterate and export transcripts for peer review.
